What Is Windsurf?

Windsurf is an AI-powered code editor that combines the familiarity of VS Code with a proprietary AI engine called Cascade — a multi-step AI agent that understands your entire codebase, not just the file you have open. It's designed to keep developers in a "flow state" by handling context-switching and boilerplate automatically.

Key capabilities:

  • 🌊 Cascade AI Agent — Runs multi-step tasks with full repo awareness
  • ⚡ Windsurf Tab — Predictive autocomplete that anticipates your next move
  • 🧠 Deep context awareness — Works on production-scale codebases, not just toy projects
  • 💳 Credit-based pricing — Simplified billing away from complex flow-action tracking
  • 🆓 Free tier — 25 credits/month to get started (no credit card required)

Who Is Windsurf For?

  • Professional developers who find Copilot too shallow and Cursor too noisy
  • Non-technical builders shipping internal tools and prototypes (as noted in Ramp's March 2026 trend report)
  • Technical founders who need to move fast and understand their entire codebase
