What is Seedance 2.0?

ByteDance (the parent company of TikTok) has officially released Seedance 2.0, their latest AI video generation model, and it's making waves across the creative industry. Accessible through the Dreamina platform and integrated into the CapCut ecosystem, this tool is poised to challenge heavyweights like OpenAI's Sora and Runway Gen-3.

Unlike previous generations that struggled with "morphing" artifacts, Seedance 2.0 excels at maintaining character consistency across frames—a massive win for storytellers.

Key Features

1. Hyper-Realistic Motion

The fluid movement understands physics better than most competitors. Hair, fabric, and water interact naturally.

2. Character Consistency

Upload a reference image, and Seedance keeps the character recognizable throughout the clip. Finally, your protagonist doesn't change faces every 3 seconds.

3. CapCut Integration

Seamless export to CapCut for editing, effects, and sound design. This is the killer feature—you don't need five different apps anymore.

How to Access Seedance 2.0

Currently, the model is available via the Dreamina platform, but the best way for creators to leverage this tech is through the **CapCut Creative Suite**. By using CapCut Pro, you gain access to the broader ecosystem of AI tools that ByteDance is rolling out.
