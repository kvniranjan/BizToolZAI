What Is OutlierKit?

OutlierKit is a web-based YouTube research and analytics platform focused on outlier detection — identifying videos that dramatically outperform expectations relative to a channel's subscriber count. Instead of guessing what will work, you get data showing you what's already working.

This is a fundamentally different approach from tools like VidIQ or TubeBuddy, which primarily help you optimize SEO after you've chosen your topic. OutlierKit helps you choose the right topic before you ever hit record.

Key differentiators:
