Vibespace

What is it?

Vibespace is a platform that emphasizes local multi-agent creation for apps and workflows. It allows you to build custom AI agents that work together locally, giving you strong control layers over your automation processes.

Who is it for?

Builders, developers, and operational teams needing custom, secure agents to automate complex workflows and apps without relying solely on cloud-based processing.
