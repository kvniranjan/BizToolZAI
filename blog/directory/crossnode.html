What: A platform that packages automations into client-facing, billable services seamlessly.

Who: Marketing agencies, consultants, and B2B SaaS providers looking to monetize their internal automated workflows.

Crossnode is a game-changer for service businesses wanting to productize their offerings. Instead of just using automation internally, Crossnode allows agencies to wrap their AI workflows into secure, client-facing interfaces that can be monetized. It handles the infrastructure, billing, and access control, allowing you to turn a complex automated marketing or reporting sequence into a scalable, self-serve subscription product. This enables an entirely new revenue stream with minimal overhead.
