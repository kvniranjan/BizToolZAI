Glide

What: A no-code platform that turns existing data into lightweight, functional business apps, emphasizing speed, automation, and accessibility.

Who: Marketers, non-technical founders, and ops teams needing custom internal tools without writing a single line of code.

Price: Free tier available; paid plans scale with usage and features.

Link: Visit Glide

Glide empowers business users to bypass the engineering bottleneck. By simply connecting a spreadsheet or a database, marketers and operators can instantly generate polished, mobile-friendly applications for inventory tracking, CRM, or client portals. It incorporates AI to streamline data mapping and app logic, allowing teams to prototype and launch robust internal workflows in minutes rather than months. It is the perfect bridge between raw data and actionable user interfaces.
