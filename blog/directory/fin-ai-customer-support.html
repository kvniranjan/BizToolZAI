What is Fin AI?

Fin AI, developed by Intercom, is a breakthrough in automated customer support. Unlike old-school chatbots that frustrate users with rigid decision trees, Fin uses advanced LLMs to understand complex queries and resolve them using your existing help center content. It claims a massive deflection rate by accurately answering questions before they ever reach a human agent. Fin integrates seamlessly into the Intercom ecosystem, meaning it learns from your team's history and adopts your brand's tone instantly.

Who is it for?

SaaS companies, high-volume e-commerce stores, and any business using Intercom for support will find immense value here. It is designed for Support Leads looking to reduce ticket volume without sacrificing customer satisfaction scores (CSAT). If your team is buried in "how-to" questions, Fin is the filter you need.

Pricing & Links

Fin AI is priced per resolution (ticket solved), meaning you only pay when it actually does the work. It is an add-on to existing Intercom plans.
