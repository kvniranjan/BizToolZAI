Klu

What is it?

Klu is an internal search engine powered by AI. It unifies scattered company knowledge, meeting outputs, and documents into a single, easily searchable hub. It saves your team countless hours trying to find information that is buried in Slack, Google Drive, or Notion.

Who is it for?

Ideal for growing teams, business operators, and SaaS companies struggling with knowledge fragmentation across different apps.
