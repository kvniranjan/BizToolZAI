50 AI Prompts for Business Professionals

Stop staring at a blank page. Here is a curated collection of copy-paste prompts for email, strategy, and analysis.

The difference between a generic AI answer and a useful one is the Context. These prompts are designed to give the AI a role, a task, and constraints.

Cold Outreach
Act as an expert B2B sales copywriter. Write a 3-email cold outreach sequence to [Target Persona] at [Industry] companies. The goal is to book a 15-minute demo of [Your Product]. Constraint: Keep each email under 100 words. Use a friendly but professional tone. Focus on the pain point of [Specific Pain Point].
Difficult Client Update
Draft an email to a client explaining that the project deadline will be delayed by 3 days due to [Reason]. Tone: Empathetic but firm. Structure: 1. State the news directly. 2. Explain the reason briefly (no excuses). 3. Propose the new delivery date. 4. Offer a small value-add to make up for it.

Pre-Mortem Analysis
We are about to launch [Project Name/Description]. Act as a pessimistic risk analyst. Conduct a "Pre-Mortem" analysis. Assume the project has failed 6 months from now. List the top 10 likely reasons why it failed, categorized by (Technical, Market, Operational). For each risk, suggest one mitigation strategy.
Pareto Principle (80/20)
I want to learn [Skill/Topic] as fast as possible. Apply the Pareto Principle (80/20 rule). What are the 20% of sub-skills or concepts that will give me 80% of the results? Create a 4-week learning plan based on this.
