What is Jasper AI?

Jasper (formerly Jarvis) is an AI writing assistant built specifically for marketing and business content. Unlike general-purpose AI tools, Jasper is designed to:

  • Learn and maintain your brand voice
  • Generate marketing copy at scale
  • Integrate with your team workflow
  • Provide templates for specific content types

It's powered by a combination of AI models (including GPT-4 and their own fine-tuned models) and wrapped in a purpose-built interface for content teams.
