The Problem: Editing Takes Forever

If you have a podcast or a YouTube channel, you know the pain. You record for an hour, but turning that into 10 TikToks takes another 5 hours of editing, captioning, and cropping.

This "distribution bottleneck" prevents most creators from growing.

The Solution: Munch AI

Munch (GetMunch) is an AI repurposing engine. You feed it a long video link (YouTube or upload), and it spits out 10-20 short, vertical clips that are ready to post.

But it's not just random cuts. Munch uses GPT-4 to analyze the content and pick the "most viral" moments based on current trends.

Key Features

1. AI Curation

It finds the hooks, the jokes, and the insights automatically. It understands context, so it won't cut you off mid-sentence.

2. Auto-Captions

The captions are 95% accurate and styled like popular creators (Alex Hormozi style) to grab attention immediately.

3. Virality Score

It assigns a score to each clip predicting how well it will perform on TikTok/IG Reels based on trending topics.

4. Platform Native

It automatically crops your horizontal video to 9:16 vertical, keeping the speaker centered using face detection.

Final Verdict

For podcasters and agencies, Munch is an absolute no-brainer. It effectively automates your social media strategy. The time saved on editing clips is worth 10x the monthly cost.
