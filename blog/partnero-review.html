What is Partnero?

Managing partners, tracking attributions, and handling payouts can be a nightmare without the right infrastructure. Enter Partnero, an AI-powered partnership management platform designed to automate the heavy lifting.

It combines affiliate management and referral programs into one dashboard, built specifically for SaaS companies and E-commerce brands looking to reduce reliance on paid ads.

Key Features

1. AI-Powered Automation

Partnero distinguishes itself with "Partnero AI," which helps launch and grow programs. It takes the guesswork out of program structure and partner discovery.

2. Flexible Commission Structures

Whether you need recurring commissions for SaaS subscriptions or one-time rewards for e-commerce sales, Partnero handles it. It supports custom cookie durations and tiered rewards.

3. Automated Payouts

One of the biggest pain points in affiliate marketing is paying your partners. Partnero streamlines this with automated payout management.

The Partnero Affiliate Program

If you are a content creator, agency owner, or consultant, promoting Partnero itself is a lucrative opportunity.

  • Commission: 50% on the first sale + 30% recurring monthly commission for 24 months.
  • Second Tier: Earn an extra 10% from the earnings of affiliates you refer.

Final Verdict

For SaaS founders looking to implement a "product-led growth" strategy via partnerships, Partnero is a modern, robust choice. Its AI capabilities and slick UI make it 2026-ready.
