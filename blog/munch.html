Munch AI - Auto-Pilot Video Repurposing

Creating content is only half the battle. Distributing it efficiently is where many creators struggle. Enter Munch AI, the cutting-edge video repurposing tool that takes your long-form videos and automatically extracts the most engaging clips for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

With its intuitive dashboard and customizable templates, Munch takes the heavy lifting out of editing. We tested Munch on a 30-minute podcast episode and within minutes, we had 10 highly-optimized shorts complete with auto-captions and trend-focused edits.

Key Features

  • Auto-Cropping & Tracking: Keeps the subject in frame.
  • Trend Analysis: Identifies segments with the highest viral potential based on current social media trends.
  • Auto-Captions: Highly accurate and stylized text overlays.

Verdict: If you want to scale your content output without hiring a full-time editor, Munch AI is a must-have tool in your 2026 tech stack.
