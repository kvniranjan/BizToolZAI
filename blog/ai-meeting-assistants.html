The Problem with Meetings

You're in a meeting. You're trying to listen, but you're also frantically typing notes. By the time you write down one point, you've missed the next two. It's a broken system.

AI meeting assistants fix this. They join your Zoom/Teams/Meet calls, record audio, transcribe it, and‚Äîmost importantly‚Äîsummarize it into key takeaways.

1. Fireflies.ai (The Powerhouse)

Fireflies isn't just a transcription tool; it's a knowledge base for your meetings. It connects deeply with your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot) and project management tools (Asana, Monday, Trello).

Key Features

2. Otter.ai (The Original)

Otter is the tool that started it all. It's excellent at pure transcription and has a very clean, simple interface.

Key Features

  • Real-time Transcription: Watch the text appear as people speak (great for accessibility).
  • OtterPilot: Auto-joins meetings and emails summaries.
  • Slide Capture: Automatically grabs screenshots of slides presented during the meeting.
