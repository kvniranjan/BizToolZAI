Best AI Meeting Assistants 2026: Granola vs Fireflies vs Otter

Are "Meeting Bots" dead? We compare the new wave of AI notepads (Granola) against the heavyweights (Fireflies, Otter) to see which one actually saves you time.

The Shift: Bots vs Notepads

For years, AI meeting tools worked the same way: a bot joined your Zoom call, recorded everything, and sent a transcript. It worked, but it was intrusive.

Now, a new category is emerging: AI Notepads. These tools (like Granola) run locally on your machine, don't invite bots, and feel more like a magic notebook than a surveillance tool.

But are they better than the powerhouses like Fireflies? Let's compare.

1. Granola: The "Anti-Bot" Choice

G

Granola

Best for: Individuals, Founders, Consultants

Granola is different. It's an app on your Mac. It listens to your system audio (so it works on Zoom, Teams, Slack, etc.) without joining the call. You can type bullet points during the meeting, and Granola uses them to structure the final AI summary.

  • No awkward bots: Your client doesn't know you're using AI.
  • User-guided notes: The AI follows your lead on what's important.
  • Beautiful output: Notes look hand-written, not robotic.

2. Fireflies.ai: The Powerhouse

F

Fireflies.ai

Best for: Teams, Sales, CRM Integration

Fireflies is a beast. It records video and audio, transcribes everything, and pushes data directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, or Slack. If you're managing a team or need a "single source of truth" for company meetings, this is it.

  • Deep Integrations: Auto-log calls to your CRM (huge for sales).
  • Conversation Intelligence: Analyzes sentiment, talk time, and topic trends.
  • AskFred: Chat with your meeting history ("What did we decide about pricing last month?").
