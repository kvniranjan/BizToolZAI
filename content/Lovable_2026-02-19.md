### The Test: Building a CRM

*   **Prompt:** "Build a dashboard with a list of properties, a map view, and a lead capture form."

### Lovable vs. Cursor
This is the big question.
*   **Cursor:** Is an IDE for *developers*. It helps you write code faster. You still need to know what a `useEffect` hook is.
*   **Lovable:** Is for *founders*. You don't need to know code. You just need to know what you want to build.

### Verdict
If you are a developer, stick with Cursor. It gives you control.
If you are a founder/entrepreneur who just wants the product *done*, **Lovable** is currently the best tool on the market. It feels like magic.
