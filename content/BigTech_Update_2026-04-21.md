### 🔴 OpenAI: Trying to be Your Entire OS

Sam Altman’s crew isn't just playing in the chat box anymore. They are setting up shop in your IDE and your backend.

*   **Agents SDK is Here:** OpenAI finally rolled out its new Agents SDK. Why do you care? Because "Agent" isn't just a buzzword anymore. It brings a model-native harness and native sandbox execution. **The Founder Play:** You can now safely let OpenAI workflows execute files, tools, and code natively without setting your dev environment on fire. It's time to build those autonomous internal tools you've been putting off.
*   **Codex Goes Turbo:** Codex just got marketplace support, MCP (Model Context Protocol) capabilities, and a new secure devcontainer profile. It's an app-server in a box. **The BA Angle:** When your engineers say "that will take three sprints," you can point them to the new Codex. 
*   **GPT-5.3 Instant Mini & The $100/mo Pro Tier:** ChatGPT gets a new fallback model that's actually, well, good. And there's a new $100/month Pro tier. Start budgeting for it now, because it's bundled with those sweet new Codex usage options.

### 🔵 Google Gemini: Making It Way Too Easy to Switch

Google is playing the "We have your data, let us use it better" card, and honestly? It's working.

*   **Personal Intelligence Goes Global:** The 'Personal Intelligence' upgrade is now rolling out globally to Gemini Ultra, Pro, and Plus users. It gives Gemini a deeper memory and understanding of *you*. **The BA Angle:** Stop re-explaining your company's ridiculous KPI structure every time you open a new chat. Gemini now actually remembers what you care about.
*   **The Ultimate Troll Move (Chat Imports):** Google just added cross-platform chat imports. Meaning? You can literally export your ChatGPT history and dump it into Gemini so you don't lose your custom prompts. **The Founder Play:** If you've been hesitant to test Gemini because of "switching costs," Google just paid the toll for you.
*   **Gemma 4 Drops:** Need local open-weight models? Gemma 4 (specifically the 26b and 31b-it versions) just dropped on AI Studio. For the privacy-obsessed founders building local-first tools, this is your new sandbox.
