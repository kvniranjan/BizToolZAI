*   *The BA/Founder Impact:* You are no longer writing Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for humans to ignore. You are deploying an agent to scrape the data, format it in Notion, and sync it to Dropbox (which, by the way, OpenAI also just added native write capabilities for).

## Google: Gemini 3 Pro and Enterprise Agent Platform

Not to be outdone, Google woke up and chose violence. They launched the **Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform** (the next evolution of Vertex AI). It is a comprehensive suite to build, scale, govern, and optimize AI agents across an enterprise. 

Accompanying this is the new **Gemini 3 Pro model**. Google claims it boasts a 50% improvement over 2.5 Pro in solving benchmark tasks, specifically targeting depth, reasoning, and developer tool reliability.
*   *The BA/Founder Impact:* If OpenAI's agents are the scrappy startup employees, Google's Enterprise Agents are the corporate consultants in suits. For enterprise-grade founders or BAs looking to automate secure, governed, scalable workflows across Google Cloud, this is your new playground.
