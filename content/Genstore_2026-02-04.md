# Genstore Review: Building Stores with Autonomous Agents

In Feb 2026, **Genstore** stormed the charts, hitting #1 on Product Hunt. The promise? "From Idea to Live Store in Minutes" using **autonomous agent teams**.

## What is Genstore?
Genstore bills itself as an "AI-Native Commerce" platform. Instead of dragging and dropping website builders, you deploy a team of AI agents. You give them a concept, and they handle the execution.

## Key Features
*   **Autonomous Agent Teams**: Specialized agents for design, inventory, and copy.
*   **Speed**: Launch a fully functional store in under 10 minutes.
*   **Integration**: Connects with major payment gateways and shipping providers instantly.

## Verdict
If you're looking to test e-commerce concepts rapidly without the technical debt of Shopify or WooCommerce setup, Genstore is a must-try.
