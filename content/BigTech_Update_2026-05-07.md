# The AI Vibe Shift: GPT-5.5 Instant & Google's Multimodal Catch-Up

*By Joanna Stern (Style)*

Look, I know what you’re thinking. "Another AI update? Do I really need to care, Joanna?" 

Yes. Yes, you do. Put down the overpriced iced coffee and let's talk about what just happened in the past 48 hours. OpenAI and Google both just dropped updates that are actually going to change how you do your job this week. 

## OpenAI Just Dropped GPT-5.5 Instant 
(And thankfully, it's less cringe).

OpenAI just replaced its default model with **GPT-5.5 Instant**. The big pitch? "A new class of intelligence for real work." In plain English: It's faster, it scores an 81.2 on the AIME math test (up from 65.4), and most importantly, they've finally trained it to stop using so many gratuitous emojis. 

**What this means for Founders & BAs:**
* **Accuracy over Fluff:** If you use ChatGPT to draft pitch decks, analyze market data, or clean up Excel formulas, you'll notice fewer hallucinated numbers and significantly less fluff.
* **Codex on AWS:** For the technical founders, Codex is now on Amazon Bedrock. You can finally build and deploy AI workflows within the security of AWS.

## Google's Counter-Punch: Multimodal RAG
Google didn't want to be left out of the news cycle. The Gemini API File Search tool just got three major updates: **multimodal support, custom metadata, and page-level citations.** Also, Google Home gets a Gemini chatbot upgrade, but unless you're trying to automate your smart blinds to close when you start crying over a spreadsheet, we don't care about that right now.

**What this means for Founders & BAs:**
* **Page-Level Citations are the holy grail.** If you're feeding massive PDFs (like competitor 10-Ks or market research reports) into Gemini to build a custom RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) tool, it will now tell you *exactly* what page it got the answer from. 
* **Multimodal Search:** You can finally search across text *and* images in your company's messy internal knowledge base.

## The Verdict
Switch your ChatGPT default to 5.5 Instant right now. And if you're building an internal search tool for your startup, it’s time to take a hard look at the Gemini API's new citation feature. It might just save you from pitching hallucinated data to a VC.
