## What is Opus Clip?

Opus Clip is a generative AI video tool that repurposes long videos (like podcasts, webinars, or reviews) into professional short clips. It doesn't just cut; it analyzes the content to find "hook" moments, adds dynamic captions, and reframes the shot for vertical viewing (9:16).

## Key Features in 2026

1.  **AI Curation Score:** The AI rates the potential virality of each clip it generates, so you know which ones to post first.
2.  **Active Speaker Detection:** It automatically keeps the speaker in the center of the frame, even if they move around.
3.  **Auto-B-Roll:** (New!) Opus can now insert relevant stock footage or generated visuals to cover slow moments.
4.  **Custom Branding:** Upload your fonts, logos, and color palettes to keep every clip on-brand.
