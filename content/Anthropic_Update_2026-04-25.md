# Claude's New Superpower: Making Charts Before You Can Finish Your Coffee
*A Weekly Feature Review*

Listen, I love a good spreadsheet as much as the next business analyst, but let's be real—turning rows of data into a chart that doesn't look like it was designed in 1995 takes time. Time you could be spending, I don't know, actually *analyzing* the business.

Well, this week, Anthropic just dropped a feature for Claude that is going to save you so many clicks: **Custom charts and inline visualizations**.

Yes, you read that right. No more exporting CSVs, opening Excel, highlighting columns, and wrestling with the chart wizard. Claude can now generate visual charts directly inside its responses.

### How to use it like a pro:

1. **Dump the Data:** Paste your raw data (sales figures, Q1 user growth, whatever you've got) right into the Claude prompt.
2. **Ask for the Visual:** Try a prompt like: *"Take this quarterly revenue data and give me a bar chart showing the month-over-month growth. Make it look sleek."*
3. **Behold the Magic:** Claude will analyze the data and output a crisp, inline visualization right there in the chat window.

**Why Business Analysts should care:**
This isn't just a party trick. It's about speed. When stakeholders ask "How did the new product line do in Q3?", you don't need to build a whole deck. You just ask Claude, grab the inline chart, and drop it in Slack. Boom. You look like a data wiz, and you're back to drinking your coffee before it gets cold.

Currently rolling out to Max and Pro plans. Get ready to never touch a pivot chart again!
