## Google: Faster, Cheaper, and... Predictive?

First up, Google quietly dropped **Gemini 3 Flash Preview**. Google claims it delivers "frontier-class performance" at a fraction of the cost, but the real kicker? Upgraded *agentic coding capabilities*. It's not just writing code; it's practically trying to deploy it for you. 

But wait, there's more. Google is also rolling out "Proactive Assistance." Yes, you read that right. Gemini is testing features to predict your needs *before* you even ask. 

**What this means for Founders/BAs:**
*   **Cost-Effective Scaling:** If you're building AI into your app, Gemini 3 Flash is begging to be your cheap, fast backend engine.
*   **The "Anticipation" Factor:** Proactive Assistance could automate routine reporting. Imagine Gemini just pulling your weekly SaaS metrics before your 9 AM Monday sync because it *knows* you need them.

## OpenAI: ChatGPT Wants Your Notion Access

Over at OpenAI, they’ve realized that chatbots are useless if they can’t actually *do* things. ChatGPT (especially Business tiers) just got updated apps for **Box, Notion, Linear, and Dropbox**. 

Here’s the massive change: **Write capabilities.** ChatGPT isn't just reading your Notion docs anymore; it can actually write into them. Plus, word on the street is the new fallback model, **GPT-5.3 Instant Mini**, is making free and basic interactions much more conversational. 

**What this means for Founders/BAs:**
*   **Workflow Automation Unlocked:** If you're a PM or BA, ChatGPT can now digest a user interview and immediately write the tickets into Linear or the PRD into Notion. No more copy-pasting.
*   **The Seamless Stack:** This is OpenAI trying to become your operating system. Connect your docs, and let the AI do the administrative heavy lifting.

We are officially moving from "chatbots that summarize" to "agents that execute." Your action item this week? Go into your ChatGPT settings, connect Notion or Linear, and see if it can draft your next sprint plan. And if you're building, get on the Gemini 3 Flash preview and test those API costs.
