## What is Seedance 2.0?

Seedance 2.0 is a text-to-video and image-to-video model designed for high coherence and motion stability. Unlike previous generations that struggled with "morphing" artifacts, Seedance 2.0 excels at maintaining character consistency across frames—a massive win for storytellers and content creators.

### Key Features:
*   **Hyper-Realistic Motion:** Fluid movement that understands physics better than most competitors.
*   **Character Consistency:** Upload a reference image, and Seedance keeps the character recognizable throughout the clip.
