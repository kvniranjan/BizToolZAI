## Review Body

### The Problem with Traditional Embedded Analytics
For years, SaaS companies have struggled with "buy vs. build" for analytics. Traditional BI tools like Tableau or PowerBI are powerful but clunky to embed. Custom builds are expensive and hard to maintain.

### Enter Qrvey: The AWS-Native Solution
Qrvey (pronounced "vey" like "survey") takes a different approach. It's built *specifically* for SaaS companies running on AWS. This isn't just a dashboarding tool; it's a complete data strategy in a box.

### Key Features that Matter
1.  **Agentic AI Integration**: In 2026, static charts aren't enough. Qrvey's "agentic analytics" allows users to ask questions and get answers, not just visualizations.
2.  **AWS Native Architecture**: If your stack is on AWS, Qrvey deploys directly into your environment. No data egress fees, better security, and seamless scaling.
3.  **Multi-Tenancy Built-In**: Most BI tools treat multi-tenancy as an afterthought. Qrvey handles row-level security and tenant isolation out of the box.

### Who is this for?
This is a high-leverage tool for:
- **Product Managers** who want to add premium analytics tiers to their SaaS.
- **CTOs** looking to offload the maintenance of custom analytics stacks.
- **Business Analysts** who need to define data models that scale across thousands of customers.

### Verdict
If you're building a B2B SaaS on AWS, Qrvey is likely the most architectural-sound choice on the market right now. It moves beyond simple charts into genuine data automation.
