# n8n Review 2026: Why It's Still the King of Customizable Automation

In the rapidly evolving landscape of SaaS in 2026, where interoperability between AI models (like ChatGPT/Claude) and productivity suites is paramount, **n8n** stands out as a critical infrastructure tool. Unlike traditional "black box" automation platforms, n8n offers a unique node-based visual editor that appeals to both developers and power users.

## What is n8n?

n8n (nodemation) is a workflow automation tool that lets you connect apps and services together. It's often compared to Zapier or Make (formerly Integromat), but with a key twist: it's **fair-code** and can be self-hosted, or you can use their managed cloud version.

## Key Features in 2026

*   **AI Integration**: Seamlessly connect LLMs like GPT-5 and Claude 3.5 directly into your workflows for intelligent data processing.
*   **Visual Workflow Editor**: The node-based UI is intuitive, allowing you to drag and drop connections and see exactly how data flows.
*   **Self-Hostable**: Privacy-conscious businesses love that they can host n8n on their own servers, keeping sensitive data within their control.
*   **Fair-Code License**: Free to use for internal business purposes if self-hosted (with some limitations), or pay for the convenience of n8n Cloud.

## Pricing

n8n Cloud offers tiered pricing, often more competitive than Zapier for high-volume tasks because they don't charge per "step" in the same restrictive way. The **Pro** plan is particularly popular for small teams automating their entire backend.

## The Verdict

If you need simple, linear automations, Zapier is fine. But if you're building complex, branching logic or need to process heavy data loads with AI, n8n is the superior choice. Its flexibility is unmatched.
