# How to Use Claude's New Inline Charts (And Impress Your Boss)

*By Ravi, in the style of Joanna Stern*

Look, we've all been there. It's 4:30 PM on a Friday. Your boss wants a "quick" breakdown of Q3 sales trends, and you're staring at a CSV file the size of a phone book. Usually, this means crying into an Excel pivot table. 

But not this week! Anthropic just dropped a lifesaver for Business Analysts everywhere: **Claude can now create custom charts and inline visualizations right in its responses.** 

Yep, no more "Here is the data, go make your own chart" cop-outs.

### The Magic Trick

Here's how to use it to save your afternoon:

1. **Upload your data:** Drop that messy spreadsheet, CSV, or even a copied-and-pasted table straight into Claude.
2. **Ask for the visual:** Don't just ask for a summary. Type something like: *"Analyze this data and give me a bar chart showing revenue by region for Q3, plus a line chart for the month-over-month trend."*
3. **Watch the magic:** Boom. Claude spits out actual, readable charts right in the chat window. 

### Why BAs Should Care

*   **Speed:** You go from raw data to a visual presentation in seconds.
*   **Customization:** You can say, "Make the bars blue and add a trendline," and it just... does it.
*   **Context:** It doesn't just give you a picture. It gives you the *analysis* right next to the chart. You can literally screenshot the response and drop it into a Slack update.

**The Verdict:** This is a huge win for anyone who spends their days translating data into English for executives. It’s like having an intern who actually knows how to use graphing tools. Try it out on your next data pull. Your Friday afternoon self will thank you.
